\subsection{Orientations}
We let $\O$ be the set of all orientation lines (ie. all signed densities) on a fixed vector space $E$.
\begin{defn}[Orientation]
	An orientation of $E$ is given by a choice of a vector $or\in \O$.
\end{defn}
